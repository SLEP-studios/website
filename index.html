Games made by SLEP Studios

SLEP Studios is a game development team founded in early 2024 by JWO, DLY and KRM.

The most recent game published by SLEP Studios is Candy for Life, a 3D halloween game. You can find more info on this at the SLEP Studios Youtube channel.

Other games made by Slep studios include:

Evolving Cubos and the Risen Kingdom - a work-in-progress 2D platformer

Press Space - fly through space and protect your planet!

Cube Adventure - a fun retro-like game in your browser
